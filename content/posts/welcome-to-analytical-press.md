## A quick note before we begin

Analytical Press is a small thing. Two friends, one sport, a shared suspicion that the commentary cycle has not been kind to the way we actually watch matches anymore. We would rather sit with a question for a week than publish twice on Tuesday.

You will find two kinds of work here:

- **Essays**, where we write out the thing we would otherwise try to make fit in a two-paragraph group chat message.
- **Analytical projects**, where the numbers earn their keep. We post full notebooks when the notebook *is* the piece.

Everything is free, nothing is algorithmic, and the front page is the most recent post. If you have been sent a link, welcome. If you found your way here by accident, also welcome.

## Why a site, and why now

There are perfectly good places to read about soccer. None of them, we noticed, were *us* writing *to each other*. We have enough shared jokes and half-formed arguments between us to justify pulling them into the open.

It is also, plainly, easier to cite yourself later if the thing lives on a domain.

## What to expect

A post lands when one of us finishes something we are proud of. That means you might see two in a week and nothing for a month. We will trade off between essays and data work. Nothing here will ever go behind a paywall, and nothing here is written by committee.

If you want to know who we are, the [About](/about) page is where we explain ourselves. If you want to read the backlog, the [Archive](/posts) has every piece in reverse chronological order.

Thanks for reading.
